Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services? We have answers.

What areas do you service? ▼

We proudly serve Florence, Darlington, and Hartsville, South Carolina. We're based in Florence and have been caring for local properties for over 15 years. If you're unsure whether your address is in our service area, please call us at (843) 555-0192 and we'll let you know right away.

Do you offer year-round service? ▼

Yes! Our services adapt to the seasons. Spring and summer feature regular lawn maintenance and landscape design work. Fall includes leaf cleanup and pruning preparation. Winter is typically lighter but we're still available for emergency cleanup and planning. We're open Monday to Friday 8am-6pm and Saturday 9am-2pm.

How much does a free estimate cost? ▼

Nothing! Our estimates are completely free with no obligation to hire us. We'll visit your property, discuss your vision, and provide a detailed quote with pricing. We typically respond to estimate requests within 24 hours. Use the contact form on this page or call us directly.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? ▼

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with our work, we'll make it right. We stand behind our services and have built our 15-year reputation on doing quality work and treating our clients with respect. We've earned a 4.9★ average rating because we don't leave until the job is done to your satisfaction.

Do you handle large commercial properties? ▼

Yes! We work with both residential and commercial clients. Whether you need lawn maintenance for a downtown office building, landscape design for a new business, or irrigation installation for your property, we can handle projects of any size. Commercial clients appreciate our reliability and professionalism. Contact us to discuss your commercial needs.

Are you licensed and insured? ▼

Absolutely. We're fully licensed and insured to protect you and your property. You can hire with confidence knowing that any issues or accidents are covered by our insurance. We take professionalism seriously and maintain all required licenses to operate in South Carolina.
